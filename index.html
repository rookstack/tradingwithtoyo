FAQ

Do I need trading experience?
No. We explain every signal so beginners can follow along and learn. That said, you should understand basic options mechanics before trading real money — our education library covers this.

Signals and VIP members receive 3–5 momentum options plays per week. We only post when the setup is clean — quality over quantity.

Any options-approved brokerage works — Robinhood, Webull, Tastytrade, TD Ameritrade, etc. We don't require a specific platform.
